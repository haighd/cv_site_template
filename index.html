More About Me

Dan is passionate about helping others, whether developing RPA bots to automate tasks for his team-members, to building data pipelines between a variety of organizational data sources (e.g. SAP, data lake products, BlueYonder TMS web application, Manhattan WMS/IBM Cognos, etc.), often culmanating to bespoke interactive dashboards, applications, and/or machine learning/predictive models. Dan draws from his experience working with a variety of organizations, from non-profit healthcare systems, to Fortune 4 publicly-owned businesses, having led Six Sigma/Process Improvement projects and Data Analytics projects, oftentimes resulting in over $1M annual hard-dollar cost benefit, for those organizations.

Dan holds a Master of Engineering in Healthcare Systems Engineering, from Lehigh University, as well as a Master of Science in Analaytics, from Georgia Institute of Technology. Until recently, Dan also held a Paramedic certification, from the State of NJ, having provided critical care and advanced clinical interventions to the citizens of Northern New Jersey. Dan often credits the 20 years he spent provided that care, with fostering the development of his critical thinking skills, while exposing him to new experiences and learning opportunities.

When not hunched over a computer screen, Dan enjoys spending time woodworking, reading the latest fantasy epic, and spending time with his wife and 3 dogs.
